Happy Ending Play School

About Us

Welcome to Happy Ending Play School, a joyful place where every child learns, plays, and grows with love and care. We believe that early childhood is a magical time filled with curiosity and creativity. Our safe and colourful classrooms, fun-filled activities, and caring teachers help children build confidence, make friends, and enjoy learning every day. At Happy Ending Play School, every little step is a big celebration of happiness and growth.

Our Vision

To create a happy, safe, and inspiring environment where children learn with joy and grow into confident, caring individuals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture every child's curiosity through play-based learning, creative activities, and personal attention, ensuring overall development.

Why Choose Us?

  • Safe and child-friendly environment
  • Experienced and caring teachers
  • Play-based and activity-oriented learning
  • Focus on physical, mental, and social development
  • Fun and colourful classrooms

Our Activities

We provide storytelling, drawing, colouring, music, dance, indoor games, outdoor play, and creative activities that help children learn naturally while having fun.

Programs We Offer

  • Play Group
  • Pre-KG
  • KG
